# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at simon@baerlocher.ch. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

For the full text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
